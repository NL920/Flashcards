This is a main page of my flashcards application. For now it is meant to be only for french flashcards - as I am eagerly learning this beautiful language.

This project was developed as an educational project to demonstrate web application development using the Flask framework.

  • Flashcards: Practice your vocabulary with randomly selected flashcards. Enter the correct translation and receive instant feedback to reinforce your learning.
  • Add: Create new flashcards by entering an French word and its corresponding translation. Newly added flashcards are immediately available for future study sessions.
  • Delete: Remove flashcards that are no longer needed or contain incorrect information. This helps keep your vocabulary collection organized and up to date.
  • Is there...?: Search for a specific word to verify whether it already exists in the flashcard collection. This feature helps prevent duplicate entries and makes it easy to find saved vocabulary.
